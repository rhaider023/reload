S e r v i c e s

Reload is a service-based company that provides physiotherapy, messages therapy and personal training using the Pilates methodology. The company offers options for individual sessions, specific packages for each service or combined packages. Sessions can be purchased online directly from the company's website.


Physiotherapy

Our physiotherapists are passionate about healthcare and rehabilitation, going the extra mile to recover health and physical performance. Clients are actively involved in their recovery process, armed with the tools, training and knowledge necessary to recover from their injury. Our team focuses not only on rehabilitation but also on returning to sports, training and injury prevention. Extended treatment sessions and uninterrupted one-on-one care enable patients to get the most out of every physiotherapy treatment session at Reload.
