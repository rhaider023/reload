About Us Reload is a physiotherapist-owned based in Calgary that has as main characteristic the individualized care of our clients. We aim beyond the rehabilitation and treatment of orthopedic injuries. Injury prevention and technical skills to improve performance are extremely important during the rehabilitation process. At Reload, the client is accompanied throughout the rehabilitation process. Our team of physiotherapists, Pilates instructors, and massage therapists have specialization in orthopedics, sports and high-performance training.

Reload's goal is not simply rehabilitation, but also the search for a return to sports activities and the pursuit of a better physical and mental performance.

At Reload, you will have a personalized service by professionals of high level who also practice sports and know the routine of athletes and active people.

We believe that the best way to achieve health, performance, and quality of life is to build on long-term care, avoiding "quick-fix" treatment and focusing on an active treatment, where the client is part of the process of rehabilitation and also learn how to care effectively.
