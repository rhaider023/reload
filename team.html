Team


Physiotherapists

Martha Thompson PT, MScPT (Registered Physiotherapist)

Martha is a 2002 University of Calgary graduate with a Masters in Physical Therapy. Martha has a specialization in Australian Manual Therapy and the McKenzie Institute certifies her in McKenzie Method. Martha's approach is a combination of manual therapy, exercise and focus on the education of your clients. His style focuses on long-term treatment, injury prevention, and individualized care.


Pilates Instructors

Julia Lee, BScKIN, Stott Pilates Instructor

Julia is a Pilates instructor with background in kinesiology from the University of Calgary. With more than 10 years of experience in the fitness market and a career within the triathlon, Julia applies her knowledge focusing on maximum performance and emphasizing the importance of balancing the body through exercise. She develops exercise programs using Mat Pilates and Reformer as a basis for her work, but also accompanies our clients who seek better aerobic conditioning using their experience in running, swimming, and cycling.


Massage Therapists

Debbie Smith, RMT (Registered Massage Therapist)

Debbie graduated from the Canadian Therapeutic College in 2010, and since then works as a massage therapist for a wide variety of conditions such as lymphatic drainage, deep tissue, post surgical, and trigger point therapy.
